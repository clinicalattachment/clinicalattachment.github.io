Clinical Attachment Programme for IMGs

6-week hands-on programme tailored for International Medical Graduates

Overview

Location: NHS-affiliated General Practice, Hoyland Medical Practice, Barnsley

Duration: 6 weeks (extension possible upon agreement and availability)

Start Dates: Flexible monthly intakes throughout the year

Supervision & Experience

Supervised history-taking, exams, and NHS consultations with real patients

Mentorship from GP Partners, PLAB 2 OSCE Examiners, and an Educational Supervisor

Certification & CV Support

Certificate of Completion provided at the end of the programme

Opportunities to participate in audits, QI projects, and teaching sessions

Focus on UK-based clinical guidelines and ethics
