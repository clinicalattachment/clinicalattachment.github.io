Clinical Attachment Programme for IMGs

A Fully Supervised, Hands-On NHS Experience

Overview

This 6-week Clinical Attachment Programme is designed for International Medical Graduates (IMGs) seeking firsthand exposure to the UK healthcare system. It offers structured and fully supervised clinical experience in an NHS GP practice.

Programme Structure

  • Supervised hands-on experience in history-taking and clinical exams
  • Exposure to real NHS consultations and case discussions
  • Training in documentation, prescribing, and NHS models
  • Personalised learning plans with targeted improvements
  • Participation in audits and QI projects
  • Home visits, minor surgery, and MDT observation

Additional Features

  • Regular feedback and reviews
  • Career guidance: GP training, CV, and interview prep
  • Accommodation help available

Who Should Apply?

  • IMGs preparing for or recently registered with the GMC
  • Doctors new to the NHS system
  • Those seeking taster weeks or practical UK GP experience
